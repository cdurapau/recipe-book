Chocolate Mug Cake

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • pinch of salt
  • 3 tablespoons milk
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • splash vanilla extract
  • 3 tablespoons chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Mix flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt into mug.
  2. Add the milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla and blend until smooth.
  3. Stir in chocolate chips.
  4. Microwave on high for 90 seconds.
  5. Let the cake rest for 2 to 3 minutes before eating.
